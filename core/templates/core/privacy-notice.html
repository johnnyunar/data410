Privacy Notice

Your privacy is our utmost priority. At Data410, we do not collect, store, or process any personal information. This notice provides full transparency about the minimal data we handle and how we use it.

Overview

Data410 is designed to give you full control over your digital privacy. We operate with a strict no data collection policy. This means:

  • No personal data is collected, stored, or shared.
  • We only track anonymous page visit counts for performance monitoring.
  • All features are designed to work without requiring any identifiable information from you.

What We Track

To improve our website and ensure it works seamlessly, we track the total number of page visits. This data is:

  • Aggregated and anonymous, with no personal identifiers attached.
  • Used solely to monitor website performance and usage.
  • Stored securely and not shared with any third parties.

We do not use cookies or any tracking mechanisms that collect personal information.
